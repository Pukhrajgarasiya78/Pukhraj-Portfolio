Hi, I’m
Pukhraj Garasiya
& Data Scientist

Hello! My name is Pukhraj Garasiya, and I am from Pokharda town of Nimuch. I am currently pursuing a B.Tech specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, a field that perfectly combines my lifelong love for mathematics and my newfound passion for coding and data.

From a young age, I was fascinated by the world of numbers. My interest in mathematics grew even stronger when I started helping my friends with their math questions and received positive feedback. This encouragement led me to choose a combination of maths and science in my 11th grade, further deepening my love for the subject.

As I delved deeper into the world of numbers and patterns, I discovered the exciting realm of coding. This new interest led me to specialize in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, where I could merge my analytical skills with cutting-edge technology. Over the years, I have worked on various projects, honing my expertise in data science, machine learning, deep learning, and data analytics.

My technical skill set includes proficiency in Python, SQL, C++, Java, R, Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Visualization, Statistical Analysis, Data Analysis, MySQL, Tableau, Power BI, Excel,HTML ,CSS,JavaScript. I have applied these skills in numerous practical projects, from analyzing data to building predictive models. I am passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights and solutions that can drive real-world impact.

In addition to my academic and technical pursuits, I enjoy collaborating on innovative projects and sharing knowledge with the tech community. Whether it's developing machine learning models, exploring deep learning techniques, or creating interactive data visualizations with Power BI, I am always eager to tackle new challenges and expand my horizons.
